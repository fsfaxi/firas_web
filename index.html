About Me

Highly motivated, hardworking and ambitious engineer. Always looking forward to develop new skills and widen my scope of knowledge. I was born and brought up in Tripoli; the capital of Libya. I have done my 3 years of high school study majoring in engineering science in a secondary school near the city center. Since then, my passion towards technology and being an engineer started to grow.

After that, I decided to move to Malaysia to take my bachelor study in telecommunications engineering. Then I decided to dream big and pursuit the master degree of engineering in telecommunications in the same university.

During my engineering study, I was introduced to a new world full of enthusiasm and interesting challenges; The world of programming !. Since then, I became a huge fan of programming and decided to learn more programming technologies. During the trimester break, I got the opportunity to take a web development fundamentals course offered by the university and I didn't hesitate for a moment to take it !

Ever after, I moved back to Libya and worked for a year as a web developer. And now I'm moving to Australia to begin a new adventure and start new successful career.
